About PromptBase

PromptBase is an open-source, community-driven project for sharing, saving, and discovering high-quality prompts. It is built to stay simple, transparent, and owned by its contributors rather than by a company.

Open Source

This project is released under the MIT License. You are free to use, modify, distribute, and build upon it with minimal restrictions.

The source code, issues, and future roadmap are publicly available on GitHub. Contributions, forks, and critical feedback are welcome.
